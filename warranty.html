Warranty & Returns

Last updated: 22 October 2025 • UK customers

What’s covered

12-month Return-to-Base (RTB) warranty on complete PCs covering parts & labour against defects in normal use. Every system is thermally tested before shipping.

  • Parts & labour: If a component fails under normal use, we repair or replace with same or better spec.
  • Proof provided: We log stress tests (OCCT/3DMark) and can supply results on request.
  • Software: Windows activation and drivers are included; we provide remote help for setup issues.

Change-of-mind returns

  • Standard builds: 14 days from delivery (unused, as-new, all accessories/packaging). Buyer covers return shipping.
  • Custom PCs/cases: No change-of-mind returns (bespoke). Faulty-on-arrival rights still apply.

Faulty on arrival (within 30 days)

If something is wrong when you receive it, contact us within 30 days. We’ll diagnose and arrange repair or replacement per UK consumer law.

What’s not covered

  • Physical damage, liquid damage, incorrect mains power, or damage from shipping due to inadequate re-packaging.
  • User modifications outside guidance (e.g., aggressive overclocks, BIOS mods, unsupported part swaps) that cause failure.
  • Normal wear (fans, thermal paste), cosmetic marks after use, or software/data loss (always keep backups).

How to start a warranty claim

  1. Email bobsbuildsuk@gmail.com with your order details, description, and photos/video of the issue.
  2. We’ll run remote diagnostics. If a return is needed, we’ll issue an RMA number and packing guidance.
  3. Return to base: You ship the PC to us (insured). We cover outbound shipping back to you after repair/replacement.

Tip: Keep your original packaging; it’s safest for transit. Remove loose accessories before shipping.

Ownership & transfer

The 12-month warranty is tied to the PC and is transferable to a new owner within the original period (please share the original proof of purchase and contact details).

Your statutory rights

This policy is in addition to your rights under UK consumer law. It does not affect those rights.

FAQs

Do you use new parts?

Yes, unless clearly stated otherwise for a special build. We keep invoices for major components.

How long do repairs take?

Most are completed within 3–7 working days after the PC arrives with us, subject to parts availability.

Will I lose my data?

We try to avoid wiping drives, but repairs may require re-installation. Please maintain your own backups.
