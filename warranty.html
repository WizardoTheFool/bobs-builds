Warranty & Returns (Plain English)

Warranty: 12-month Return-to-Base warranty covering parts & labour for defects in normal use. We provide remote diagnostics; if a part fails, we repair or replace with same or better spec.

Change-of-mind: Standard builds: 14 days (unused, original condition). Buyer covers return shipping. Custom-made PCs/cases: no change-of-mind returns. Your statutory rights for faults still apply.

Faulty items: If something’s wrong, contact us within 30 days and we’ll arrange repair/replacement per consumer law.
